Hello, I'm Jorge Squire, a passionate oil painter inspired by emotion, texture, and light. My work focuses on capturing human connection and natural movement in a timeless, traditional style.

Each piece is an invitation to pause, reflect, and experience the beauty of nature and the complexity of the human spirit. I work primarily in oils, drawing influence from classical techniques while adding my own modern perspective.
