Jorge Squirestrong> is a contemporary oil painter whose work is deeply influenced by emotion, light, and texture. His practice centers on capturing the nuances of human connection and the natural world through a traditional yet timeless approach to painting.

Working primarily in oils, Squire draws inspiration from classical techniques while incorporating a modern sensibility that reflects his unique artistic voice. His compositions are characterized by their subtle movement, layered atmosphere, and a contemplative stillness that invites reflection.

Each painting serves as a visual narrative—an opportunity for the viewer to pause, engage, and connect with the emotional and environmental landscapes he portrays. Jorge Squire continues to explore new themes while remaining committed to craftsmanship, storytelling, and the enduring power of visual art.
