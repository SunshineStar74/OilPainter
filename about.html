Jorge Squier is a local Houston artist who has taken inspiration from his travels, and embracing his own creativity, he began painting scenes of the peoples and landscapes that he encountered. Squier's art is a reflection of his experiences and a way to share his unique perspective with others. This page is dedicated to showcasing and selling Squier's art. Please take a moment to explore his work and feel free to contact him with any questions or inquiries.

Squier created this art page to showcase and sell his art. Partial proceeds from each piece of art sold are donated to charities focused on children, education, and providing food for those in need.
